Our Ideal Jobs

If you have come to this page you’ve already met our team and read a little about each of us.
Here we are comparing and contrasting of each team members ideal jobs.
In general, our ideal jobs were all very similar. Software engineer/developer was the direction that
most of the group wanted to head with their careers with the exemption of one military job being cyber analyst.
We all planned to be working in large organisations but ones with very different services and platforms.
The largest differences in our ideals would have to be the geographic locations of each.
In the below table you will find a comparison of each of our ideal jobs and the career paths we hope will get us there.
